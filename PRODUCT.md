# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Software architects, developers, human-resource people looking for both the arc42 template and training, as well as general software-architecture information.

## Product Purpose
To provide high-quality resources for software architecture, promote the arc42 template, and facilitate registration for specialized architecture training courses. Success looks like users easily finding the information they need and seamlessly registering for courses.

## Brand Personality
Expert, reliable, trustworthy.

## Anti-references
None explicitly provided. Avoid generic, untrustworthy, or overly flashy designs; prioritize clarity and professionalism suitable for an expert engineering audience.

## Design Principles
- **Authority through Clarity**: Structure information so it is immediately accessible, reflecting deep expertise.
- **Trustworthy Professionalism**: Use a stable, reliable aesthetic that builds confidence with both developers and HR personnel.
- **Focus on Utility**: Ensure the core template resources and course registrations are frictionless and prioritized.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Follow general best practices for readability and contrast to ensure technical content is accessible. No specific strict WCAG level requested.
